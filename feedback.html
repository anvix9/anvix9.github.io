Where this goes

Your feedback is sent directly to the maintainer's inbox — no third-party platform, no public archive. It's read by a single person.

Recurring patterns across many submissions shape the next iteration of the pipeline. If you'd rather your feedback be kept entirely private, indicate that in the "anything else" field.

Honest is better than nice We can't fix what we can't see. If the document was disappointing, say so plainly — that's more useful than polite hedging. Specifics, even small ones, beat general impressions every time.

SEEKER is a multi-agent deep-research pipeline that maps the intellectual territory around a research question — building a traceable argument tree across ten or more live academic sources. It does not write your paper. It hands you the lay of the land so you can navigate it yourself.

What you'll receive

An Understanding Map in six sections: territory at a glance, intellectual genealogy, a tiered reading curriculum with active reading prompts, a conceptual map, the unresolved core, and Socratic self-assessment questions.

Plus the underlying argument tree and a verified reference list.

How it works

  1. You submit — your question lands in the queue.
  2. We run SEEKER — the pipeline searches live academic sources and builds an argument tree across three human-in-the-loop checkpoints.
  3. You receive a document — Understanding Map and brief, sent to your email within the week.
Please be patient Demand is heavy and the queue is long. You will receive your document by email within few days or within the week - sometimes sooner, occasionally later. Thank you for testing an early system.

— Example Questions That Work Well —
